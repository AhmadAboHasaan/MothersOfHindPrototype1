Where every pound goes

  • £10A meal Feeds a displaced child a hot, nutritious meal at one of our school kitchens in Gaza.
  • £25A session Covers one psychosocial therapy session with a qualified child psychologist.
  • £45A month Supports a child for a full month at a MoH primary school — schooling, meals, a nurse on site.
  • £100A teacher A week's stipend for one of our qualified teachers working in displaced‑learning centres.
  • £300A laptop Sponsors a HIEN laptop, 12 months of connectivity, and academic mentorship for a university student.
