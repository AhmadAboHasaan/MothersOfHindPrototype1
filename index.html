Who we are

An anchor for families rebuilding their lives.

Mothers of Hind is a non‑profit founded in 2024 by a group of compassionate professional mothers united by a mission for hope and humanity.

We act as an anchor facilitating educational, health and well‑being support for children and families in war‑torn areas — guided by humanitarian principles and in close collaboration with local experts and community representatives.
