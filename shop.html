Our promise

Ethical at every stage.

Fair production

Our garments use GOTS‑certified organic cotton and are produced by suppliers with fair‑trade accreditation. No sweatshops. Ever.

Considered packaging

Plastic‑free: recyclable kraft mailers, paper tape, compostable stickers. We believe small choices add up — and we will keep improving.

100% profit to mission

Every item's profit — after production, shipping and the tax we owe — is ring‑fenced for the MoH Schools, HIEN, and our mental‑health programmes.

Prefer to donate directly?

Not into merch? No problem. Every pound given directly through our donation page reaches our programmes the same way — no shipping required.
